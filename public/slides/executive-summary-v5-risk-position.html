The risk position has moved to Amber, with pressure concentrated in Operational and Customer.

The formal customer-detriment breach, repeated Cyber red, two unactioned leading risks and long-standing control gaps all point to the same weakness: escalation is visible, but ownership and delivery are not consistently keeping pace.

Page Summary

Across the page, the deterioration is concentrated rather than broad-based: one formal appetite breach remains in train; five of 42 L2 risks are red, with Cyber and Reputation lacking linked remediation; four material and one key control are ineffective; and 53 tests remain overdue. Three remediation actions are in flight, but two are at risk of delay. Taken together, the commentary indicates that risk identification is working, but ownership, action coverage and evidence of sustained control effectiveness are not yet keeping pace with the areas under greatest pressure.
